Telco Customer Churn Prediction

End-to-end classification pipeline on the Kaggle Telco dataset — cleaning, EDA, encoding, and model comparison to predict which customers are likely to churn.

  • Cleaned and explored ~7,000 customer records; handled categorical encoding with get_dummies(drop_first=True)
  • Stratified train/test split with StandardScaler normalization
  • Compared Logistic Regression against Random Forest as a baseline
